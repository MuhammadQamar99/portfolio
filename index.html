Ask Me Anything
Q. How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites take 5-14 days depending on complexity. Simple 5-page websites are usually done in a week. Complex sites with custom features take longer. I always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Q. Why are you so affordable?

I'm 17 years old from Pakistan. I have no agency overhead, no office rent, no team to pay. I work directly with you from my home. Plus I use modern AI tools that make me work faster. You get senior-level quality at beginner-friendly prices.

Q. Do you accept international payments?

Yes! I accept payments via Payoneer (works like a US bank account), Wise transfer, and bank transfer. Pakistani clients can pay via Easypaisa, JazzCash or bank transfer. I require 50% deposit to start work.

Q. What if I don't like the design?

I provide unlimited revisions until you're 100% happy. I show you progress at every step so we catch issues early. Your satisfaction is guaranteed — if you're not happy, I keep working until you are.

Q. Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Every website I build is mobile-first. That means I design for phones first, then make it work on tablets and desktops. 70% of your visitors will be on mobile so this is critical.

Q. Do you provide hosting and domain?

I help you set up free hosting on Netlify or Vercel (perfect for small business sites). For domains I recommend Namecheap or GoDaddy. I'll help you connect everything so it just works. Total cost: ~$15/year for domain only.

Q. Can you update my website later?

Yes! I offer monthly maintenance for $50 (Rs. 5,000). This includes content updates, new images, adding new pages, fixing issues and keeping everything running perfectly. Or you can pay per-update if you prefer.

Q. How do we communicate during the project?

WhatsApp is fastest and easiest. I respond within hours during the day. We can also use email, Zoom calls, or whatever works best for you. You get direct access to me — no project managers or middlemen.
